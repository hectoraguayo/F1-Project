What's new in formula 1 - 2023

The brand-new

Just in case you weren't absolutely raring to go for the first Grand Prix of the 2023 season, here's something that will put you in the mood for some Formula 1... It's the brand-new Formula 1 title sequence that you'll see before crucial sessions in 2023 – complete with the goosebump-inducing F1 theme music.

Add to that some sparkling new graphics depicting all 20 drivers on the grid, including rookies Nyck de Vries, Logan Sargeant and Oscar Piastri. Furthermore, there's Fernando Alonso in his new green Aston Martin race suit, Pierre Gasly in Alpine colours, and Nico Hulkenberg sporting Haas's race gear. So have a seat, crank the volume up, and watch the brand-new F1 titles in the video player above.

73 years of being a sensation

Formula 1 is a high-speed, high-tech motor racing competition held on circuits around the world. It is considered the premier class of motor racing and has a large number of fans and enthusiasts around the world.

Formula 1 is an exciting and spectacular competition, combining the most advanced technology with the skill and talent of the world's best drivers. If you are passionate about high-speed racing and technological innovation, Formula 1 is definitely a competition you won't want to miss.
